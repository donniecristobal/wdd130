Overview

Purpose

The goal of the website is not only to have an efficient, appealing, and engaging web presence, but also to stem the subsequent most important objectives. To encourage aspiring commercial models in this field. To make it easier for professional casters to find available and existing models. To respond to frequently asked questions that website visitors may have. To display galleries of past and current projects, workshops, and awards. To promote and share information about the services available as well as the experiences of previous customers. To provide accurate and up-to-date rates, schedules, and upcoming promotions. To provide methods for corporate personnel to plan their marketing and advertising strategies. Finally, provide potential customers with a location address and contact information.

Audience

Audiences for this website are aspiring commercial models, professional casters, marketing and advertising personnels, fashion designers, bloggers, photographers, web admins and developers.
